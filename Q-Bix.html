Q-BiX

Project Overview

Q-BiX games take the concept of the 2D fighting genre with a more casual and friendly approach for all ages. The game is designed for mobile devices, especially Androids, so players can enjoy exciting fights on the go through their mobile devices. In this game, players will control a box-shaped character with a minimalist yet unique design. Players will fight in different arenas, facing other characters in challenging battles, but still easy to understand for everyone.
