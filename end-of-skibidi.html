End of Skibidi

Project Overview

The game titled "End of Skibidi" will take the genre of 2D platformer games with a touch of RPG adventure. The game is designed for the Android platform, allowing players to access and play it anywhere using their mobile devices. In this game, players will take on the role of a cameraman who is tasked with fighting and destroying the main enemy, namely Skibidi Toilet. Players will go through a variety of challenging levels and exciting adventures. The main character in this game is a cameraman who has to fight various kinds of Toilet Skibidi that threaten the peace of the city. To avoid being boring, this game is designed with various levels that have diverse settings and unique enemy designs. The Toilet Skibidi in this game has different appearances and special abilities that make each fight interesting and challenging.
