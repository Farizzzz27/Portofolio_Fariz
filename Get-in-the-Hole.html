Get in the Hole

Project Overview

Get in the Hole is a 3D platformer game with arcade elements that immerses players in the exciting experience of controlling a ball in a challenging world. In this game, players are given full control of a ball that must be moved through various levels with a unique and varied path design. Each level is filled with winding tracks, sharp turns, ramps, and dynamic obstacles that make each game session a new challenge.
